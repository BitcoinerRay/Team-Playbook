# AI Collaboration Principles

## When to Use AI

Use AI for: scaffolding, boilerplate, repetitive patterns, first drafts, and exploration. Do not delegate final decisions, security-sensitive logic, or irreversible actions without human review.

## Prompt Quality

Prompts should be specific, include context, and define expected output format. Vague prompts yield vague results. Reference standards and examples when available.

## Human Review Gates

AI-generated code, docs, and designs require human review before merge. Treat AI output as a draft, not a final artifact. Review for correctness, security, and alignment with team standards.

## Agent-Consumable Structure

Content intended for AI consumption should be atomic, well-structured, and machine-parseable. Use consistent formats, clear input/output specs, and explicit examples. Avoid prose-only documentation for executable workflows.
