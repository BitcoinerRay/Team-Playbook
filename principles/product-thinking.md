# Product Thinking Principles

## Problem-First

Start with the problem, not the solution. Validate that the problem is real and worth solving before building. Avoid solution-looking-for-a-problem.

## Outcome Over Output

Measure success by outcomes (user behavior, business impact) not output (features shipped, lines written). Define success metrics before implementation.

## Scope Control

Explicitly define in-scope and out-of-scope. Non-goals are as important as goals. Resist scope creep by documenting boundaries and revisiting them when requests arrive.
